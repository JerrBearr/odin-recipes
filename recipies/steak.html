How to make the perfect sweet marinade for tough steaks

Perfectly cooked filet, marinated to juicy deliciousness

Overview: What to expect

Making a marinade is easy! All you've gotta do, is combine a fat, with an acid, and add flavor! It may take you longer to assemble the ingredients, than to actually make the marinade.

First you need to know which type of steak you want to cook. If using a tougher cut of meat, like a tri tip, then you'll need to let it sit for longer to pentrate and do its magic. If using something like a filet, or a New York, then 24-48 hours is plenty of time for marination. I will leave a tri tip in the fridge marinating for close a week! Just make sure it is fully submerged, and in a sealed bag. If the bag inflates at all, your meat has gone bad!

Ingredients

  • White balsamic vinegar
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Fresh pineapple, mango, and passionfruit if you have access
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder
  • Diced onions and garlic
  • Worcestershire sauce

How to make

  1. Chop onions and garilc
  2. Add to blender
  3. Add all liquid ingredients
  4. Blend all ingredients and spices
  5. Add chopped fruit, blend until even consistnecy

    6. NOTE: Do not add too much fruit. You just want a litte flavor, and especially with passionfruit, a little goes a long way.

    PS: If you pan fry your steak, you can use a little red wine and reduce it and make a sauce to pour over your steak. That is what I did in the above picture.
