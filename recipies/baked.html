Baked Burritos

Overview: what to prepare for

This is a relatively easy recipie that is great for a low effort delicious dinner. Easily scaleable to whatever your needs are. When the burritos are done, and this cannot be stressed enough, THEY WILL BE HOT! LET THEM COOL!

Feel free to add any additional protien, or vegetables and herbs based on your tastes!

Personal Preferred Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Rosarito jalepeno flavored refried beans
  • Cilantro
  • Shredded cheese
  • Tapatio

How to make

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425F
  2. Heat beans and combine with tapatio and cheese in microwave until melted
  3. Place bean mixture inside tortillas, add fresh cilantro inside. Place on baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes, or until outside of torillas begin to change color or become crunchy
  5. THEY WILL BE SCREAMING HOT. LET THEM COOL FOR AT LEAST 5-10 MINUTES.
